OU Bloggers - At a Glance

Yesterday, I was part of a DigiLab session on HE institutional blogging. Martin gave a good presentation on why people should be blogging (ThOU Shalt Blog; the presentation should be is on Slideshare anytime):

I rambled about some of the technology around blogs:

- readers: Bloglines;
- blog publishing/editors: Blogger(Wordpress was mentioned too);
- productivity tools: Scribefire;
- ranking/linking: Technorati;
- syndication/stats: Feedburner;
- comment tracking: CoComment;
- tagcloud navigation: Zoomclouds.

Mulling over how we could promote 'access' to people blogging about OU issues, I thought I'd add a tab to the OUseful Pageflakes site:

Just go to the OUseful dashboard, select the OU Bloggers tab and you'll get an overview of blogs from people who blog regularly on OU related issues.

(If you think your blog should be on that page too, mail me... :-)
