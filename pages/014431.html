I've been thinking about the social life of URLs today, and in particular about people commenting on Twitter about URLs, so that I can add these comments into the mix along with delicious comments and blogs comments about a URL (see for example the deliURL pipe - http://pipes.yahoo.com/ouseful/deliurl).
