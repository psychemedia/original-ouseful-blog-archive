Almost October OU Jobs Roundup

As there have been quite a few interesting looking job ads released here over the last few days, I thought I'd do another update:

  • Systems Programmer(s), Technical Services, Computing Services: "We are looking for an experienced systems programmer to work in the Technical Services Section. Whilst developing and maintaining our core IT infrastructure you will play an important role in the on-going success of our 24*7 University"

  • Leading Systems Programmer (Network Development), Technical Services, Computing Services: "We require a network analyst with experience in network systems design and management. The successful candidate will provide leadership and management in a team of 4 people providing network development services to the University."

  • Leading Systems Programmer (Messaging and Servers), Technical Services, Computer Services: "We require a systems programmer with experience in the areas of e-mail, server and storage management. The successful candidate will provide leadership and management in a team of 4 people providing messaging and server infrastructure services to the University."

  • Media Developers, Learning & Teaching Solutions (LTS) - Three 12-month contracts: "LTS is the media production centre of The Open University. We are looking for additional media professionals to develop web materials for The Open University's Centre for Continuing Professional Development (CCPD).
    "You will have experience in either the editing or graphic design of web materials, and be familiar with web development tools and production processes. You will also be skilled in working, as part of a team, with a range of media developers and content writers. Good presentation, communication and organisational skills are essential. Knowledge of creating interactive multimedia materials, such as Flash animations, is a requirement."

  • Media Project Manager, Learning & Teaching Solutions (LTS): "LTS is the media production centre of The Open University. We are looking for additional media professionals to develop web materials for The Open Universityâ€™s Centre for Continuing Professional Development (CCPD).
    "Do you have an excellent record of managing media production and building successful relationships with clients? You will lead a team of media developers working on web-based distance-learning materials for the CCPD.
    "Reporting to the media account manager, you will manage the development of learning materials from early planning through to the finished product. You will work closely with academic and media production staff on specifications, product development and delivery of course materials to quality, time and budget."

  • Research Fellow: Context-sensitive and Multimedia Search, Knowledge Media Institute (KMi) : "KMi has an opening for a Postdoctoral Research Fellow to undertake research within the context of an EPSRC-funded project, Renaissance, which aims to investigate context-sensitive information retrieval based on quantum theory, with applications to cross-media search and structured document access. Based on Professor Keith van Rijsbergen's pioneering work in quantum-theory based framework for information retrieval, this is a joint project between the Open University, the University of Glasgow and Queen Mary University of London. The Open University will mainly focus on contributing to the development of a unified framework for the representation, evolution and interaction of contexts, implementing and evaluating the framework in large scale multimedia search tasks, such as TRECVID and INEX Multimedia Track."

As ever, I have nothing to do with any of the above, but if you're up for interview, get one of the posts etc etc, and I'm around at the time, ping me and let's go for coffee ;-)
