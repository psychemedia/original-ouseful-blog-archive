Interactive Google Advermaps - OU Walton Hall Campus

In a post about OU Regional Offices on Google Maps, I demoed an official OU regional offices map, along with an unofficial Yahoo mapmixer map that overlaid an OU campus map onto a Yahoo map.

I also linked to an evolving interactive OU campus map that I keep tinkering with (and which apparently has had over 7000 views...).

On that note, Stuart Brown recently posted a YouTube movie that the comms team put together showing a Walton Hall campus tour.

It's easy enough to add embedded Youtube movies to the pop-up boxes associated with push-pins on Google maps, so I added the tour to my interactive OU campus map.
