OpenLearn Unit Content Feeds via OPML

To make it easy to access collections of OpenLearn Unit content feeds (that is, the RSS versions of OpenLearn units), I've started to compile a list of Openlearn OPML feeds that bundle OpenLearn Unit Content RSS feeds in various ways...

  • OpenLearn Topics (complete listing) - http://ouseful.open.ac.uk/openlearn/opml/openlearntopics.opml (view in Grazr).
    This feed lets you view all the OpenLearn units through a single Grazr widget:

  • Courses organised by OpenLearn Topic - http://ouseful.open.ac.uk/openlearn/opml/topic/KEYWORD
    • science (or nature) - Science and Nature Courses (view in Grazr)
    • art (or history) - Arts and History Courses (view in Grazr)
    • business (or management) - Business and Management Courses (view in Grazr)
    • education - Education Courses (view in Grazr)
    • health (or lifestyle) - Health and Lifestyle Courses (view in Grazr)
    • IT (or computing) - IT and Computing Courses (view in Grazr)
    • maths (or mathematics, stats or statistics) - Mathematics and Statistics Courses (view in Grazr)
    • languages - Modern Languages Courses (view in Grazr)
    • society - Society Courses (view in Grazr)
    • studyskills (or skills) - Study Skills Courses (view in Grazr)
