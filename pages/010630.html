Fond Memories of Late Night OU TV Programmes, Relived

Looking at the OU and OpenLearn presence on Youtube just now (OU on YouTube and OpenLearn on YouTube), it struck me that the days of stumbling across OU programmes in the early hours of the morning could well be here again...

I currently have a Mogulus channel streaming techie programmes 24 hours a day, including through my FaceBook page, and it would be easy enough to do the same thing fro OU related content.

In the meantime, here's an OU Channel via Splashcast.

Add OU Snippets to your page

It contains several shows - OpenLearn, OU, Open2.net, and some flotsam and jetsam from elsewhere on YouTube...

Blogged with Flock

PS it's worth pointing out that the above channel only took a few moments to put together. Splashcast lets you cretae a show by subscribing to an RSS feed. The OU, Open2 and Openlearn shows were created in exactly that way- by entering the approriate YouTube username for each YouTube channel I wanted to subscribe to into the YouTube feed subscription slot on the definition page of each show...
