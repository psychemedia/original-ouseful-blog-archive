OUseful.Info Nominated for Edublog Awards :-)

As with Martin's The Ed Techie blog, it appears that this blog - OUseful.Info - has been nominated to the "Best Ed Tech Support" blog category of the EduBlog Awards :-) I notice that Schome Park has been nominated in the Best educational use of a virtual world, too. How's this all going to go down in the OU, I wonder?!

I'm pleased to see quite a few of the blogs on my blogroll are represented this year. In particular, Stephen's OLDaily has made it into a couple of categories (Best Individual blog and Best Resource Sharing blog) and Hey Jude in the Best Library/Librarian category.

Oh yes, voting is now open, so if you feel like casting voting for OUseful.Info, please do so :-)

(Interesting to see they're using PollDaddy to host the vote....)

Voting closes on December 6th, btw...
