OU Making Connections Conference

It's that time of year when the OU internal conference takes place (next week in fact) and this year the theme is "Making Connections" (conference website).

Trying to work out which parallel session to go to is a bit of a pain though... so given tag clouds are back in vogue, I wondered whether I could use them to help me decide which sessions to go to...

Here are a couple of examples (created by running a cut and paste of all the abstracts in a session through TagCrowd).

Day 1 - Session 1A

... and the theme of the session? Quality eLearning: New models for course design


Day 1 - Session 3A


... and the theme of the session? Quality eLearning: Using evaluations in quality eLearning


Day 1 - Session 5A

... and the theme of the session? Working with employers: The space between academe and practice


This sort of representation could make for an interesting splash projection screen to announce each session?

Also conference related, and picking up on Martin's desire to use twitter or social glue in the new IET building (Crowdstatus and filtered twitter), I wonder whether conference foyer screens showing recent tweets from conference delegates might also be an interesting idea?

(Btw - anyone noticed how blog posting frequency appears to have gone down since twitter started hitting the edublogosphere? Do the stats bear this out, I wonder? Note to self - in annual appraisal meeting, suggest that over the next year I should increase the amount of conversations I have with interesting people in bars and cafes, as measured by the number of rounds of drinks I have purchased on expenses (i.e. my beveragarati ranking ;-))
