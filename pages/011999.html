Viewing Presentations in Grazr via Slideshare RSS Feeds

I just (belatedly) picked up an email from Mike at Grazr, letting me know that Grazr will now display embedded Slideshare presentations...

Here's how one looks... The screenshot shows an embedded slidecast - so you get the audio too :-)

The Grazr widget will automatically embed the slideshow when a Slideshare RSS feed is passed into the widget.

(Grazr is generally pretty good about embedding media components that are included as feed enclosures, as my RadiOBU widget demonstrates.)

So what does this mean? Well, it's maybe one step closer to a personal, portable, learning content delivery widget?
