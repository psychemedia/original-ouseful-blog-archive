Video = 2D Photo + 1D Time?

I just added a new item to the end of my Future of Photography Splashcast show, a demo of the Recreating Movement tool that "makes it possible to extract single frames of any given film sequence and arranges them behind each other in a three-dimensional space."

If you can cope with Shockwave, there's a demo on the site that's fun to play with, though not as feature rich as the demo shown in the movie.
