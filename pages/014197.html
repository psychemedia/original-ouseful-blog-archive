The delicious history of a TinyURL

Yesterday, or maybe the day before, I posted about a Yahoo pipe that took a url and found a list of people who had bookmarked that link on delicious, and listed their description of (comments about) that link as well (Pivotal Moments... (pivotwitter?!))

I also described a pipe that would find the actual url of a page from a TinyURL.

Looking for a quick five minute tinker just now, I thought I'd plug the two together to create a pipe that takes a tinyurl andin return gives a list of who's bookmarked it - and how - on delicious:

TinyURL to delicious history pipe: http://pipes.yahoo.com/ouseful/tinyurl2delihistory

It even works...

I guess the next step is to create a simple wrapper that handles a "normal" (literal?) URL or a TinyURL, and then calls the appropriate pipe...
