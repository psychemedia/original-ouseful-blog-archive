RSS Ripples Within the OU...

For some time, I've been looking round the OU for signs of RSS being used to publish and syndicate information, both administratively, and in the context of course delivery.

Here's a quick summary of what I've found to date (note that it's not necessarily complete...).


Public Feeds

  • OU Media releases

  • Technology short course/Relevant Knowledge (unofficial rss...) course news feeds:



    I discussed these briefly here and played with here and here.

  • Faculty of Technology Phoenix/First Class conference feeds from Personal Home Pages (also available as feeds requiring authentication) - see the Service News folder on Phoenix for more information.

  • Safe Computing Bulletins.

  • OU Employment Opportunities.

  • OU library new books XML feeds


Intranet Feeds


  • OU Life Intranet news feed.


Authenticated/Password Protected feeds


  • Faculty of Technology Phoenix/First Class conference feeds available frpm subscribed to conferences (with personal login details passed via URL). An RSS read-only user can also be subscribed to conferences to allow for weak security. (Public feeds also available from Home Page conferences). See the Service News folder on Phoenix for more information.
