OUseful Feed Now Available from Feedburner

As this blog is hosted on an old, generic OU blogserver (one set up, in fact, some time ago for students on particular courses), and may be replaced as we move to our new, Moodled VLE, I'd like to encourage any readers to move their subscription to this blog over to my new Feedburner feed: OUseful Info - powered by FeedBurner

Feedburner offers a few added value services too (like its Feedflare extensions, which add Email this and Add to del.icio.us links, for example).

There's also the option to mix in a daily summary of my del.icio.us posts, which I'll look at...

However, as my posts there can be v eclectic at times, I'll perhaps see if I can just mix in summaries of posts tagged in a particular way (ouseful perhaps...)

So - if you do subscribe to this blog via the unwieldy blogs.open.ac.uk etc feed, please consider moving the subscription to OUseful Info - powered by FeedBurner

Thanks...
